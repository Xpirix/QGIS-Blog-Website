<p>It is our pleasure to announce that the QGIS.org voting members have <a href="https://www.loomio.org/d/bwdRU2Tm/adoption-of-a-new-logo-for-qgis-3-0">unanimously agreed</a> to the adoption of the proposed new logo.</p>

<p>We are currently planning the roll out of the new logo to all our applications, web platforms, and social media accounts. In addition, we will create marketing material with the new QGIS branding.  Since this is a volunteer effort, we are planning to approach this step-by-step. The goal is to have everything ready by the time of the QGIS 3.0 release.</p>
<p>If you are interested in helping with this effort, please leave a comment here and we will get in touch!</p>
