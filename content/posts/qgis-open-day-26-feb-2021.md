<p class="wp-block-paragraph">On Friday, 26 February 2021 we will be holding our monthly QGIS Open Day! What is a QGIS Open Day you may be wondering to yourself? It is an initiative to replace the wonderful community meetups we used to hold every six months when times were different. Like our in-person meetings, the event is organised on a principle of self-organisation and community participation.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">This open day will have the theme of &#8220;QGIS Plugged In!&#8221; and will be hosted, organised, focussed on and presented by young GIS practitioners from around the world!</p>

<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Where to watch</strong></p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Please see the <a rel="noreferrer noopener" href="https://github.com/qgis/QGIS/wiki/QHF-February-2021" target="_blank">event wiki page</a> for all the details of times and links for participation.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Many of the events may be recorded and made available to users who couldn&#8217;t make the live events. YouTube live streams should be automatically available for catchup viewing. Be sure to check back on the wiki page (link above) for updates!</p>
