<div>The next<b> International QGIS User Conference and Developer Meeting</b> will take place in the week from <b>4 to 10 March 2019 in A Coruña (Spain).</b></div>

<div>The call for presentations and workshop <b>registration is out and you can apply </b><a href="https://goo.gl/u5rTFV"><b>using the online registration form</b></a><b>. Note that the deadline for</b> presenting proposals is<b> 21 Dec 2018. If you need </b>any info please email your queries to:<a href="mailto:userconf2019@qgis.es"> userconf2019@qgis.es</a></div>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<div></div>
<div></div>
<div>
<div>The International QGIS User and Developer Conference wants to be the reference conference for the QGIS community, a meeting point for the family of users and developers associated with the QGIS project. Attending the conference is an opportunity to gather experience and share knowledge about QGIS. The language of the conference is English.</div>
</div>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<div></div>
<div>
<div>The event is organized by the <a href="http://qgis.es/"><b>Spanish QGIS Association</b></a> [1], the Spanish user group, and the <a href="https://xeoinquedos.wordpress.com/">Galician <b>Xeoinquedos</b> community</a> [2] with the help of <b>A Coruña municipality</b> [3]. The event is under the <a href="http://qgis.org/">QGIS.org</a> umbrella. We look forward to seeing you there!</div>
</div>
