<p>On behalf of <a href="http://QGIS.ORG">QGIS.ORG</a> and the whole community, I&#8217;m would like to extend our thanks and congratulations to all the local volunteers who helped to make the event happen, with special thanks to Luigi &#8220;Ginetto&#8221; Pirelli, who spearheaded the effort. The event, which included a full week of workshops, a user conference, and contributor meeting, marked another milestone in the history of QGIS.</p>
<p>It was possibly the largest meeting we have had to date. It especially gratifying seeing more diversity and new faces. Our inclusive and welcoming community has always been a point of pride and it was amazing seeing how newcomers easily and pleasantly integrating in the community, producing good and tangible results within just a few days.</p>
<p>Many decisions were made, many discussions were fruitfully concluded, many tortillas were eaten, plans were laid out. These events are critical for preparing the way for the future of the <a href="http://QGIS.ORG">QGIS.ORG</a> project.</p>
<p>Our heartfelt thanks to all who donated their time and intellect, and to the many donors and sponsors to make all this possible.</p>
<p>See you in Bucharest next August, for the 23rd QGIS meeting!</p>
