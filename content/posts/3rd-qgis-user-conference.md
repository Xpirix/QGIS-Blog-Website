<p>This year, we are looking forward to the <a href="https://qgis2017.wordpress.com/">3rd installment of our annual QGIS conference</a> and it will be the biggest one yet!</p>
<p>The QGIS community, developers and users are meeting from August 2–11, 2017 in Nødebo at University of Copenhagen&#8217;s Forest and Landscape College.</p>

<p><a href="https://qgis2017.wordpress.com/presentations/">The program is already online. </a>So register and make sure to get a spot in your favorite workshops!</p>
