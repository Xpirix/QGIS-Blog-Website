<p>Dear QGIS Community,</p>
<p>Our previous rounds of grant proposals have always been a great success (<a href="http://blog.qgis.org/2020/02/23/reports-from-the-winning-grant-proposals-2019/">2019</a>, <a href="http://blog.qgis.org/2020/01/06/reports-from-the-winning-grant-proposals-2018/">2018</a>, <a href="http://blog.qgis.org/2018/05/08/reports-from-the-winning-grant-proposals-2017/">2017</a>, <a href="http://blog.qgis.org/2016/10/04/winning-qgis-grant-proposals-for-2016/">2016</a>).
