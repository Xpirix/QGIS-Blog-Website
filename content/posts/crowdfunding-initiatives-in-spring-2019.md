<p>Currently there are a number of ongoing crowdfunding initiatives for improvements in QGIS that need your support:</p>
<p>1. <strong>Diagrams in print layouts, atlas and reports</strong>: the popular <a class="external-link" title="" href="https://github.com/ghtmtt/DataPlotly" target="_self" rel="noopener noreferrer">data plotly plugin</a> for interactive, exploratory charts and diagrams should be enhanced to support embedding in print layouts, atlas serial prints and reports. Details can be found at the <a class="external-link" title="" href="https://north-road.com/qgis-data-plotly-campaign/" target="_self" rel="noopener noreferrer">North Road campaign website</a>. This is a joint effort of companies Faunalia and North Road. Funding goal: € 8&#8217;600 (1&#8217;780 collected as of April 2, 2019)</p>
<p>2. <strong>Cartography proposal: selective masking of symbol levels behind labels and map symbols</strong>. Inspired by the high-quality topographic maps of <a href="https://www.swisstopo.admin.ch/">Swisstopo</a>. Details can be found at the<a class="external-link" title="" href="http://qgis.oslandia.com/en/offre-qgis/selective-masking/" target="_self" rel="noopener noreferrer"> Oslandia campaign websit</a>e. Funding goal: € 20&#8217;000 (8&#8217;000 collected as of April 2, 2019)</p>
<p>3. <strong>GeoPDF-Export</strong>: Export of georeferenced PDF files, with the possibility to measure, query coordinates, toggle map layers and query feature attributes. The project is partitioned into several work packages. This is a joint effort of Even Rouault and North Road on request of several Austrian local government agencies. For more details, please contact Johannes Kanonier of the <a class="external-link" title="" href="https://vorarlberg.at/web/land-vorarlberg/contentdetailseite/-/asset_publisher/qA6AJ38txu0k/content/atlas-vorarlberg?article_id=210182" target="_self" rel="noopener noreferrer">Landesvermessungsamt Vorarlberg</a>. Funding goal: € 30&#8217;800 (24&#8217;400 collected as of April 2, 2019)</p>
<p>4. <strong>SLYR: Converter for ESRI LYR and MXD-Files</strong>. Project is partitioned in several work packages for the conversion of ESRI styles to QGIS styles, MXD-Files into QGIS project files and ESRI layouts to QGIS layouts. Please find more details at the <a class="external-link" title="" href="https://north-road.com/slyr/" target="_self" rel="noopener noreferrer">SLYR website of North Road</a>.</p>
<p>Thank you for your support in making these projects a reality!</p>
