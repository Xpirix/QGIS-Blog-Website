<p>Last month&#8217;s user question focused on QGIS documentation. More specifically, we asked <a href="http://blog.qgis.org/2019/09/28/user-question-of-the-month-sep19/">how you learn how to use QGIS</a>. And many of you answered our call. Indeed we collected 824 responses over a period of two weeks:</p>
