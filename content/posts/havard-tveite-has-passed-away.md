<p class="wp-block-paragraph">It is with a heavy heart that we announce that on 2021-05-31, our friend and colleague Håvard Tveite has passed away at the age of 59 after a period of illness.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Håvard was a very active member of the QGIS community, providing valuable input to the <a href="https://github.com/qgis/QGIS-Documentation/graphs/contributors">documentation</a>, developing numerous <a href="https://plugins.qgis.org/plugins/user/havatv/admin">plugins</a>, and taking care of the QGIS <a href="https://github.com/qgis/QGIS-Resources">Resources Sharing Repository</a> to name just a few of his contributions.   </p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Besides his contributions to the QGIS project, Håvard was also an active volunteer in the Norwegian Orienteering Federation and in the International Orienteering Federation Map Commission (more: <a href="https://orienteering.sport/norwegian-great-havard-tveite-has-passed-away/">https://orienteering.sport/norwegian-great-havard-tveite-has-passed-away/</a>)</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">The QGIS community would like to extend our heartfelt condolences to Håvard’s wife Ingrid and son Nils.&nbsp; Håvard’s contributions to the QGIS Project will leave a lasting impact on the lives of many people around the world as they benefit from the work he has done on a daily basis.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">R.I.P. Håvard</p>
