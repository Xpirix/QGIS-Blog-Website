<p class="wp-block-paragraph">We are pleased to announce that the <a href="https://qgis.org/community/foundation/agm-minutes/QGIS-AGM2024-Minutes.pdf">minutes from the <strong>QGIS.ORG Annual General Meeting (AGM) 2024</strong></a> are now available for public review.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Since the establishment of <strong>QGIS.ORG</strong> as a formal legal entity in 2016, we have held virtual AGMs to ensure transparent governance. These meetings allow QGIS Voting Members to <strong>approve the annual budget, review financial reports, elect new project members</strong>, and make other key decisions affecting the future of the project.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Key Highlights from the 2024 AGM</h2>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">The AGM took place virtually from <strong>November 20 to December 1, 2024</strong>, with discussions held via mailing lists and voting conducted through online forms.</p>
