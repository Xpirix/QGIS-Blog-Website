<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Highlights of the planned improvements include: </p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Point Clouds</strong><br>Creating point cloud processing tools for transformation, management and analysis of point clouds. Ensuring that extremely large (terabyte size) datasets can be handled well for both display and analysis.</li>



<li><strong>Elevation Profiles</strong><br>Support embedding customisable elevation profiles into print layouts and atlases, and allow exporting elevation profiles to CSV and DXF.</li>



<li><strong>3D Maps</strong><br>Faster 3D maps for large scenes, an improved 3D measurement tool and further improvements to 3D scene navigation.</li>
</ul>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Your financial support is vital to make these improvements possible!
